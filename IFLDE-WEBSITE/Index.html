E-RTU

eRTU is the official Learning Management System (LMS) of Rizal Technological University. It is an online platform that allows students and teachers to manage and participate in classes digitally. Through eRTU, users can access lessons, submit assignments, take quizzes, view grades, and join forums anytime and anywhere. It supports flexible, distance, and blended learning, ensuring continuous education even beyond traditional classrooms. The system is built using Moodle, a widely used e-learning software that promotes accessible, interactive, and student-centered learning.
